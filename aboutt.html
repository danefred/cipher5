ABOUT US

This Cipher-5 aims to explore a wider exchange and availability of official weather information on the internet. In view of this, this website is established as a centralized source on the Internet for media to access official weather information issued by the Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical, and Astronomical Services Administration (PAGASA) efficiently and effectively. The Cipher-5 website is developed and maintained by the said agency.

This website presents OFFICIAL weather observations, weather forecasts, and climatological information for selected cities or provinces supplied by the PAGASA nationwide. The PAGASA makes official weather observations in the different provinces of the country. Links to their official weather service websites and tourism board/organization are also provided whenever available. Weather icons are shown alongside worded forecasts in this version to facilitate visual inspection.

VISION

Cipher-5 provides world leadership and expertise in international cooperation in the delivery and use of high-quality, authoritative weather, climate, hydrological and related environmental services by its members, for the improvement of the well-being of societies of all nations.

MISSION

Cipher-5 works to facilitate worldwide cooperation in the design and delivery of meteorological services, foster the rapid exchange of meteorological information, advance the standardization of meteorological data, build cooperation between meteorological and hydrological services.

GOAL

Cipher-5 aims to better serve societal needs, Enhance Earth systems observations and predictions, and advance targeted research and reliable weather information, , and encourage research and training in meteorology because weather is the most important local news topic
