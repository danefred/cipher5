About Us

This Cipher-5 aims to explore a wider exchange and availability of official weather information on the internet. In view of this, this website is established as a centralized source on the Internet for media to access official weather information issued by the Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical, and Astronomical Services Administration (PAGASA) efficiently and effectively. The Cipher-5 website is developed and maintained by the said agency.
